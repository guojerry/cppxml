English Player: The handy listening tool!

In playing mode, you can press F7 to pause or continue current playing, press F8 to replay the just played sentence, press F9 to slowly replay the just played sentence, and press F6 to jump to the previous sentence.

In dictation mode, it will automatically stop at every sentence end and replay it once. You can press F7 to continue to next sentence, press F8 to repeat current sentence, press F6 to jump to previous sentence, press F9 to repeat slowly current sentence. Check the "Dictation Mode" in the toolbar to enable dictation mode.

Wish you enjoy your good time in english tour with EnglishPlayer accompanied.
